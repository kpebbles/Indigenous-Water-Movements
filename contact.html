Who am I and why am I making this website?

I am a Ph. D. student at Michigan State University in the Writing, Rhetoric, and American Cultures (WRAC) department. I am studying writing and rhetoric with a focus in cultural rhetorics and linguistics. In particular, I am interested in studying how water is perceived in communities, science, and in legislation. As a Cherokee mixed-blood who is currently living in central Michigan and lived in Flint, MI for over a decade, I am interested in how these perceptions help reinforce and pushback against environmental racism.
